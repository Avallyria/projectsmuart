DESIGN PROCESS

To address the limitations of traditional archaeological excavation methods, our team developed SMUART (Searching Manufactured Underground Artifacts with Refined Tools). SMUART is an integrated excavation support system that combines robotics and sensor technology to improve excavation accuracy, artifact protection, and documentation efficiency.


The system focuses on four main processes: robotic excavation, automated artifact sorting, organized artifact storage, and documentation. By combining these processes into one coordinated workflow, SMUART helps archaeologists perform excavation tasks faster, safer, and with greater consistency while reducing the risk of artifact damage and human error.
